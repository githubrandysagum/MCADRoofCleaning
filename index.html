Our Services

Roof Cleaning

We will assess the roof in and out and then decide the best course of cleaning for your requirements and then give you a quote.

Patio Cleaning

We do all types of patio, stone and block paving cleaning.  We use different chemicals for different surfaces.  We have been fully trained by Pure Seal in the use of chemicals.

Render and Wall Cleaning

We do soft washing or very low pressure steam washing of your render and biociding so as to get rid of the organic growth which is on your render.  The results can be amazing as our pictures show.

Gutter Cleaning & Repairs

We use a Gutter Vac so can deal with most heights (up to 40Ft) We undertake all kinds of repairs and can replace if needed. Making sure your gutters and downpipes are clean also helps to prevent any damage and damp to your walls.

Why moss cracks tiles?

By cleaning the moss off your roof, you are protecting not just your roof but also your downpipes, soakaways, and gutters, which could become damaged and cause costly repairs.
